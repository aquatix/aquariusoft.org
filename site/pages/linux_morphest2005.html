Where and how?

It boils down to the fact that I'll host the fun, as Alextreme's place is a bit too cramped to house the number of people. I live half an hour [with public transport] north of Amsterdam [in Heemskerk FYI].

Sleeping

I will have sleeping room for about 7 people -- a number we seem to have reached now -- maybe even more people will fit if everybody lines up neatly and don't mind sharing the same floor :) [However, I want to take 7 as a max for the moment]. If possible, bring your own sleeping bag and other stuff. If even more people want to attend, we can always try to find a [cheap] hotel in the neighbourhood.

Stuff to bring

Of course, bring along digicams, laptops [I've got wifi and an adsl connection] and other gadgets/toys and stuff. Sleeping gear like a sleeping bag, your favorite rug or something to sleep on may be handy too.

If you need something that is best bought here, let me know.

Update @ 2005-11-20: At this moment it looks like we have 5 regular mattresses, and one slim one. Furthermore, we have two [smallish] couches and a stretcher. About 7 sleeping bags and some blankets are accounted for too. Olivier, Marc, maybe can you bring your own mattress or something by car?

Update @ 2005-11-26: I just hope everybody fits :P First night will be spent at clubs and pubs in Amsterdam anyhow :)

Location

As stated, I live in Heemskerk, a small city north of Amsterdam. I will mail you my exact address on request.

I've investigated what the easiest/cheapest way of getting here is, and figured the train will suite us fine. From station Uitgeest [city very near Heemskerk], I'll drive to our apartment then. Cabs are quite expensive [EUR22.50 pp], and parking costs for my car are quite high too, apart from the hassle. A train ticket is about EUR3.20 when using the reduction I get when showing my public transports card, and I know my way.
