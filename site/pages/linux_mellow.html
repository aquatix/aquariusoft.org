updated @ 2012-09-06

mellow is a Python and GTK3-based client for the Subsonic music server. It aims to create an as native desktop experience as possible, with caching of the lists for quick responses, options to cache parts of your music collection for offline playback and integration with the desktop hotkeys and sound menu MPRIS2 protocol.
