iplugs is a small collection of scripts to scrape information and show it on a kind of small portal. You can use it for building your own homepage, showing the unread count of your mboxes, movies on the [Dutch] tv, traffic jams and troubles with the railways. Note that the current version is for use in The Netherlands.
