AdBlock extension

Adblock is a content filtering plug-in for the Mozilla and Firebird browsers. It is both more robust and more precise than the built-in image blocker.

Adblock allows the user to specify filters, which remove unwanted content based on the source-address. If this sounds complicated, don't worry: it's not.

Just add a few filters. Every time a webpage loads, Adblock will intercept and disable the elements matching your filters. See?- nothing to it.

GMail notifier

If you are one of the lucky ones that have stumbled upon a GMail account, you can integrate the service into your browser with this plugin. It displays a GMail icon in the statusbar, telling you how much unread mail you've got. With one click you can open GMail in your browser.

Web services

Bloglines

Hooked on RSS feeds, but using various machines so you always have a not up-to-date unread items list? Or just not decided what aggregator you like best? Bloglines is a free online RSS aggregator. Enter the feeds of your favorite blogs, news sites and programs [or import it from your current subscriptions file] and you have your aggregator wherever you've internet access.

[ Website ]

Sitebar

Online bookmarks/favorites tool. Use one of the available services [see website], or set up your own service and have your bookmarks with you wherever you browse. It features a sidebar for your favorite browser[s] too!
