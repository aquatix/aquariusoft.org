News

Currently the most active projects are my web projects. However, the aquamorph livecd has seen a release recently too, and is under active development to get a polished livecd for general use. Also the other projects aren't forgotten either. I just have to spread my sparse free time among the things that interest me most at a given time and I don't want to spread it thin.

overload

Take a look at a work in progress: the overload RSS feedreader. Log in for a demonstration with demo/demo and be sure to check the overload project page now and then for updates. The overload project is currently being rewritten in the Django framework, which allows for a cleaner codebase and better maintainability, let alone an easier way to add new ways of accessing and storing your information.

aquamorph

One of the projects of AquariuSoft is aquamorph, a linux live-cd based on Morphix, which on it's own is based on Debian GNU/Linux.

windows programs

The old Windows programs aren't under active development anymore; detailed product pages are still missing, but there's an overview page, from which the [rather old] versions can be downloaded and a short description can be read. Mind that contact information mentioned in the programs is outdated.
